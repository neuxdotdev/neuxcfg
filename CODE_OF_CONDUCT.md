# Code of Conduct - neuxdotdev

All participants of **neuxdotdev** are expected to abide by this Code of Conduct, both online and during in-person events that are hosted and/or associated with neuxdotdev.

---

## The Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in our project and our community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:

- Age, body size, or disability
- Ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience or nationality
- Personal appearance, race, or religion
- Sexual identity or orientation

---

## The Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Referring to people by their preferred pronouns and using gender-neutral pronouns when uncertain

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants:

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or public/private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (physical/electronic address) without explicit permission
- Disrespecting reasonable communication boundaries (e.g., "leave me alone", "I'm not discussing this")
- Using sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention/advances
- Swearing or using strong/disturbing language
- Displaying graphics or content that may be considered disturbing
- Starting or participating in arguments related to politics
- Promoting inequality based on age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender, nationality, race, appearance, religion, or sexual orientation
- Promoting drugs of any kind
- Attacking personal tastes or preferences
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations of this Code of Conduct may be reported by sending an email to:

> **neuxdev1@gmail.com**

All reports will be:

- Reviewed and investigated promptly
- Handled with confidentiality
- Responded to with actions deemed necessary and appropriate

### Consequences

We hold the right and responsibility to:

- Remove comments or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Ban members temporarily or permanently for inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful behavior
